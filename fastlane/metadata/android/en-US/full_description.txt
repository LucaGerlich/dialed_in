Dialed In captures coffee bean metadata, roast notes, and brew history for specialty coffee lovers.

Keep track of your beans, roasts, and shots to dial in the perfect espresso or pour-over. Visualize your brew ratios, track extraction times, and rate your shots to improve your barista skills.

Features:
- Track Coffee Beans: Record origin, roaster, roast date, and tasting notes.
- Log Brews: Record dose, yield, time, and grind setting.
- Visualize Data: See charts of your brew history.
- Share Shots: Share your recipes with friends.
